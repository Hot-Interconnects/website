SHAHAB ARDALAN has been with Ayar Labs, Santa Clara, CA, USA,
since 2017, where he is working on high-speed SerDes link for
CMOS monolithic optical I/Os. He was with AMS R&D Group in Gennum
Corporation from 2007 to 2010. In 2010, he joined San Jose State
University as an Assistant Professor and Director the Center for
Analog and Mixed Signal where he was teaching and conducting
research on analog and mixed signal integrated circuits. Ardalan
has a Ph.D. from the University of Waterloo, Waterloo, ON, Canada.
He has published over 70 papers in integrated circuit design.
